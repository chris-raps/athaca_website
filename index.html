Our research

The Clinical World Model

At the core of our work is a neuro-symbolic Clinical World Model that simulates how real patients evolve over time under clinical, physiological, and protocol constraints. Two coordinated processing channels — a semantic track that interprets clinical narratives and reasoning patterns, and a dynamic track that models continuous-time physiological signals — integrate through layer-level alignment and cross-attention, ensuring that clinical narratives remain consistent with physiologic signals.

Unlike traditional generative models that rely solely on probabilistic correlations, our approach blends semantic understanding, temporal dynamics, and medical knowledge into a unified simulation engine. This prevents the "uncanny valley" effect where standard models generate plausible text that contradicts actual lab values — our patients are numerically and semantically synchronized.
